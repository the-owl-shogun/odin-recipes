Recipe #2: Mac & Cheese

Home Page

Ahh Macaroni & Cheese! Can there be a more comforting dish that this? Every American has grown up feasting upon this simple treat. But is it really so simple to create your own homemade version? Let's see!

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Flour
  • Whole Milk
  • Salted Butter
  • Garlic Powder
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Cook Macaroni with salt in boiling water until al dente. Drain, but do not rinse.
  2. In a separate sauce pan, create a roux by first melting the butter. Do not let it brown.
  3. Add Flower and whisk it thoroughly.
  4. After 60 seconds, add Milk and whisk.
  5. Add Sour Cream or (Greek Yogurt) and whisk.
  6. Cook this runny mixture for 10-15 minutes until sauce thickens.
  7. Add Cheese and whisk until smooth.
  8. Add cheesy sauce to the pasta, mix and serve!!!

And that's pretty much all there is to it!!!
